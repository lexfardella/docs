## Project Permissions

Project access is managed at the organization level. All members of your organization can access projects within that organization.

## Organization-Level Permissions

### Managing Team Access

To control who can access your projects:

1. Click your avatar in the sidebar
2. Select "Manage team"
3. Invite or remove team members
4. Assign appropriate roles

[SCREENSHOT PLACEHOLDER: Organization members management interface]

### Team Roles

Different team members may have different permission levels within your organization. Check with your organization administrator for specific role capabilities.

## Project Settings

### Renaming a Project

To rename a project:

1. Navigate to the project
2. Look for project settings or edit options
3. Update the project name
4. Save changes

### Deleting a Project

<Warning>
Deleting a project will remove all associated data, including project-specific documents and workflow results. This action cannot be undone.
</Warning>

To delete a project:

1. Navigate to the project
2. Access project settings or options menu
3. Select "Delete Project"
4. Confirm the deletion

## Document Access

Documents within a project are accessible to all members of your organization. There is currently no per-document permission system.

### Organization Documents
Available to all organization members across all projects.

### Project Documents
Available to all organization members when viewing that specific project.
